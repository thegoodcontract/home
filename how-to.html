THE GOOD CONTRACT

HOW TO

Welcome to The Good Contract Manual!

Thank you for choosing The Good Contract tool to create an individual employment contract that aligns with your values and desired working conditions. This manual will guide you through the process and ensure you make the most of our platform. Let's get started!

Accessing The Good Contract:

Visit our website to access The Good Contract tool.
Familiarize yourself with the user interface and available features.

Exploring Pre-Developed Paragraphs:

Within the tool, you will find pre-developed paragraphs that have been carefully crafted in collaboration with psychologists.
These paragraphs reflect various aspects of employment and can be customized to suit your needs.
Click on the provided buttons to create your personalized set of paragraphs.
Upon clicking, the selected paragraph will be added to your "The Good Contract" document, which you can view on the screen.

Understanding Testimonial Comments:

Hover your mouse over a paragraph in your "The Good Contract" to view comments derived from our survey.
These comments showcase real testimonials from women in the creative industry, highlighting the values worth advocating for.
Let these testimonials inspire and remind you of the significance of incorporating these values into your contract.

Finalizing Your Contract:

Once you have made your final selection of paragraphs, review your "The Good Contract" document to ensure it accurately represents your desired employment conditions.
If any modifications are required, use the provided editing options to make necessary changes.
When you are satisfied with the content, proceed to the next step.

Downloading Your Contract:

Click on the download button within the tool to initiate the contract download process.
Your completed "The Good Contract" will be saved as a downloadable file on your device.
Ensure you have a copy of the downloaded contract for your records.

Communication with Your Contractor:

Initiate a conversation with your contractor regarding the project and your employment terms.
Share the signed version of your "The Good Contract" with your contractor to establish a formal agreement.
Be open to receiving feedback from your contractor, whether positive or negative.

Sharing Your Experience:

We value your feedback and would appreciate hearing about your experience with The Good Contract and your contractor.
Feel free to share your feedback, experiences, and any concerns by using the Contact button on our website.
You can also participate in our campaign, offer help, seek assistance, or get in touch with us whenever needed.

Congratulations on creating your personalized employment contract using The Good Contract! We hope this manual has provided clear instructions and guidance throughout the process. Remember, we are here to support you, so don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or require further assistance.
